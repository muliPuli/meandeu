What is the EU?

The EU is a complicated political and economic union, so a lot of citizens do not know how it affects them. Fear no more, we’ll help you understand the most important ways the EU changes our daily lives!


How the EU impacts Us?

Laws

The EU is able to create laws that are valid in all of the 27 member states. These laws are created in a democratic way because the people that approve of the laws are directly elected by us, the citizens!

Programs

There are plenty of projects that are funded by the EU with a goal to improve our lives! One example is the Erasmus+ program, which gives the opportunity to students across Europe to study in another country!

Infrastructure

There are a lot of infrastructure projects that are funded by the EU to make the lives of European citizens easier! These projects can be bridges, schools, roads etc (These are normally marked with signs that show the EU flag).

Movement

Due to the EU , there is an area in which you can travel from one European country to another without a passport, just by using your ID card! Not only that, but if you are an EU citizen you can live and work in any country that is part of that area! That area is called the Schengen area!

Digitalisation

&it's threats

Digital technology is changing people’s lives. The EU’s digital strategy aims to make this transformation work for people and businesses.
